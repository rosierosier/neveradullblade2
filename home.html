Who I Am....

Never A Dull Blade, LLC, is owned and operated by me, Ricky Melton, a guy dedicated to providing the best products and services to his clients.  After over 20 years in the Professional Beauty Industry, I discovered that professional sharpening for their precious ("babies") scissors was hard to find.  There were many "sharpeners", but, few if any, understood the importance of bevel, convex, semi convex, offset, ergonomics, etc... Therefore, results were often unacceptable, or worse, the product is unusable.


More importantly, I discovered that many artist were using the wrong scissor for their comfort and long term health.
I am proud to represent the very best in Japanese scissors, Zen Master. 


In addition, I could use my skill as a sharpening craftsman to service many other industries that needed sharpening services. Including, chefs, culinary, surgical, pet grooming and quality home cutlery.
